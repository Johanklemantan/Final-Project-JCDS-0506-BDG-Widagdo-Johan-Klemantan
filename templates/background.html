BACKGROUND

Sending email is one of the alternatives used by Shopee to tell and promote certain event sale to users.
With the ability to be able to predict whether user is reading or not reading the email, enable Shopee to evaluate their crew works during campaign period until event launch.
This is because, when user is open the email, the possibility of that user know there is an event or promo will be highly increased and also increasing the possibility of that user do transaction during event period.
Therefore, with the making of this model, hopefully Shopee able to build a strategy for future marketing.

GOALS

The goals of this project is to build model that able to predict whether user is going to read the email sent by Shopee.In this case, Shopee's priority is user.

BUSINESS QUESTION

What it the criteria of user that is likely to read our promotion Email ?

What can we do in terms of email promotion campaign in order to improve the rate of checkout ?
